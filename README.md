# Life Hour Calc
A hyper-minimalist tool to convert prices into the most valuable currency you own: **your time.**

## 🚀 Concept: Time is Life
Is that new gadget really worth $1,000? Or is it worth 40 hours of sitting at your desk? 
This tool strips away the abstraction of money and forces you to face the reality of how much of your life you are trading for a purchase.

## ✨ Features
- **Zero Friction:** No login. No tracking. No ads. Just the truth.
- **Wage Persistence:** Remembers your hourly wage in your browser (Local Storage) so you don't have to re-type it.
- **Reality Check:** Provides dynamic feedback based on the labor required (e.g., "A full week of labor for this?").
- **Working Day Conversion:** Automatically calculates the cost in 8-hour working days for better perspective.
- **Privacy First:** All calculations and data stay strictly on your device.

## 🛠 How to Use
1. **Item Price:** Enter the cost of the item you're considering.
2. **Hourly Wage:** Enter what you earn per hour (after tax for best results).
3. **The Result:** Instantly see exactly how many hours (and working days) you must work to pay for it.

## 📐 The Philosophy
Money is renewable; time is not. By converting "Expense" into "Life Hours," this tool helps you make more intentional decisions about what is truly worth your limited time on this planet. Ideal for those practicing FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) or anyone looking to break a shopping addiction.
